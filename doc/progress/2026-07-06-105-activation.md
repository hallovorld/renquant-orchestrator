## Fix: fingerprint gap check

The `_fingerprint_gaps()` in `intraday_session_inputs.py` rejected runs with ANY
empty artifact hash. Optional artifacts (data_scan, meta_label, regime_conditional,
quality_floor) may not exist in every config. Changed to only require 3 core
artifacts: panel, global_calibration, ranking.panel_scoring.artifact_path.
