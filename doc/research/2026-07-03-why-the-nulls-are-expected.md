Two independent defenses, both executed:
- **Positive controls in every harness**: a synthetic effect planted in the real data must
  be DETECTED before a null is admissible. All passed: C4's 2×-bar plant → GO ×3 seeds;
  M3-verification's 8pp plant → 89% power; S9-verification's oracle → detected; C2's
  planted-decay → GO ×3; RS-5's plant → LB > +0.089; core-shrink's plant → LB +0.084 ×3;

- **Adversarial re-verification (S-REL)**: six verdicts recomputed by independent
  implementations. Zero verdicts flipped; one statistical overstatement found and corrected
  (M3's significance — which WEAKENED a rejection, i.e. the bias ran against alpha-finding
  claims, not for them).

## 2. "Our model's data is too good / it already ate the alpha" — REFUTED

If the baseline had extracted the pond's alpha, the baseline itself would show live edge.
It does not: live IC ≈ 0; S9's unconditional top-decile hit-rate = 49.96% (a coin). The
incremental-value bar (add-ons tested OVER a 158-feature baseline) is real and raises the
difficulty — but the baseline's own emptiness means the pond, not the appetite, is the
constraint.

## 3. The actual explanation — three stacked, individually-documented factors

1. **The most-fished pond.** 142 US mega/large caps: the most institutionally arbitraged
   prices in existence. Efficiency here is not a slogan; it is the null hypothesis.
2. **The most-published bait.** Quality, momentum, trend, PEAD — the exact factors with
   documented post-publication decay (McLean–Pontiff ~50%+, strongest in large caps). The
   same theory that motivates these factors PREDICTS their decay precisely where we tested.
   "Theoretically supported" is symmetric evidence: it supports both existence and crowding.
3. **Deliberately conservative gates × short windows.** The placebo-difference discipline
   (adopted after the embargo-floor incident) intentionally sacrifices slow-moving real
   signal to kill leakage artifacts — C2's PC-B proved a planted PERSISTENT tilt is cleaned
   to ≈0 by design. Power is bounded: C4's MDE ≈ 0.07 vs observed 0.033 (hence INCONCLUSIVE
   not NULL — the taxonomy matters); M3's n=8 dates; S9's BEAR cell = 1 date. Several
   verdicts are "cannot see at this n", correctly labeled as such.

## 5. The one-line ruling

The uniform nulls are the joint prediction of efficient-market theory, factor-decay
evidence, our conservative gate design, and our sample sizes. A process that kept "finding"
alpha under these conditions would be the unscientific one. The asset produced is negative

correctly instead of by hope.
